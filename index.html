#Patterns
Six systemic failures.
01

Tasks Marked "Done" Without Completion

VAs close tasks at 100% to meet KPI targets without actually completing the work. Confirmed on-record by Astrid Altuve (VA Lead) and Oriana (VA) in the March 18 meeting. KPI pressure creates a perverse incentive for premature closure.

02

No Visual QA Before Marking Done

Websites go live with wrong logos, placeholder content, old business names, and formatting errors. Kai Stone (CEO) explicitly told VAs: "you have to proof check the website." VAs acknowledged they are not doing this.

03

Websites Built Ignoring Client Specs

Sites are constructed with wrong service emphasis, wrong location details, or content that doesn't match what was discussed in the onboarding call. Client expectations are set during onboarding, then the VA builds something different.

04

Assets Not Uploaded / Work Never Started

Client-provided photos sit for weeks without being uploaded. In the Jon Crews / Royal Contractors case: "We had sent emails months ago for updates to the website and they have not all been done. We had sent a ton of pictures to be used."

05

VA Handoffs Die Silently

When one VA escalates to another, the task falls into a void. The Camila-to-Oriana pattern on Cross Country Mechanical: Camila marked it Done, then asked Oriana to fix the logo. Oriana never did. No accountability chain. No follow-up. Client lost.

06

Technical Blockers Not Communicated

VAs encounter platform bugs (CSP photo attachment issues, template limitations) but don't report them. They either skip the work silently or mark it done anyway. CSMs discover the issue only when the client complains.

#Fixes
Seven recommended changes.

These recommendations are designed to address the root causes, not symptoms. They focus on process changes and accountability structures.

P1

Mandatory Visual QA Checklist Before Task Closure

VAs must verify logo, images, content, and links match client specs before marking any website task as Done. A screenshot of the finished page must be attached to the task notes.

Owner: Astrid Altuve / Kai Stone
P1

Fix CSP Task Photo Attachment Bug

VAs report they cannot attach photos to tasks in certain cases. This is a platform bug that enables silent failures. Fix the bug so VAs have no excuse for not uploading assets.

Owner: Michael Vanhoutte / Umar (Dev)
P1

Review VA KPI Structure

The current KPI structure incentivizes quantity over quality. VAs are closing tasks prematurely to hit numbers. Oriana said this on the record. Restructure to reward quality completion, not speed.

Owner: Astrid Altuve / Michael Vanhoutte
P2

Add "Blocked" Task Status in CSP

Currently VAs have no way to flag a task as blocked. They either leave it open or mark it Done when it isn't. A "Blocked" status would make these situations visible to CSMs and leadership.

Owner: Michael Vanhoutte
P2

Pre-Launch Checklist for All Websites

No website should go live without passing a checklist: correct logo, correct business name, correct address, correct phone, no placeholder content, all client photos uploaded, all services listed.

Owner: Astrid Altuve
P2

VA Handoff Accountability

When a VA escalates a task to another VA, the original VA stays accountable until the receiving VA confirms pickup. No more silent handoffs that die in the void (Camila to Oriana pattern).

Owner: Astrid Altuve / Michael Vanhoutte
P3

Weekly Spot-Check Audit of Completed Tasks

Random sample of 5-10 "Done" tasks per week. Verify the work was actually completed. Surface patterns early before they become client losses.
